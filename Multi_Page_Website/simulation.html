Simulation Inputs

Medical Expenses i Toggle an expense to simulate using your HSA funds for that category.

When selected, the model deducts a randomly generated cost within the listed insured/uninsured price ranges to reflect different real-world scenarios and insurance coverage levels.

If your annual HSA contributions are not enough to cover the generated amount, your balance may go negative, illustrating potential out-of-pocket exposure.
